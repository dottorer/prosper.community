Prosper Academy

An online apprenticeship with a bursary for refugees

Coding bootcamps like The Firehose Project and Refugees on Rails are great at teaching people how to code, but that often isn't enough to land a dream job in the industry. A portion of bootcamp graduates have the skills to contribute to a project but lack the experience to launch a meaningful career.

Design bootcamps like General Assembly and DesignLab are great at teaching fundamentals, but lack the meaningful projects that reveal the true power of human-centered design. Most designers end up working on initiatives with no real purpose or passion.

At Prosper, we believe that people learn best by doing meaningful work with a passion.

our solution

We've designed a pay-as-you-go program where apprentices (citizens and refugees):

  • Collaborate with an interdisciplinary team on real projects within the refugee crisis
  • Meet weekly with an expert mentor and monthly with a career coach
  • Use design and technology to solve real problems for real people
  • Learn to articulate decisions to the project team
  • Gain relevant experience and contacts to land a dream job within their discipline.

Two Tracks

Because the strength of a digital worker is becoming increasingly dependant on collaboration, we've decided to run two simultaneous tracks that work together within our apprenticeship:

Design Track

Work with expert human-centered designers to solve real problems amidst the refugee crisis...

Development Track

Collaborate with experienced developers to execute designs on refugee crisis projects...

now accepting applications

We are currently accepting applications for the Prosper Academy. If you're interested, please submit your details below. We will follow-up with an interview request so that we can begin to understand you and your needs.

We understand that the needs of each apprentice is different, and that's why we don't prescribe a single curriculum but rather allow real projects to form the basis of experiential learning.

We also provide specialized training for our mentors who work with apprentices that come from challenging circumstances so that we can focus on building holistic well-being, self-confidence and employability.

Volunteer

You can make a difference in the lives of refugees by offering your skills to a meaningful project

Whether you're able to offer an hour a week or ten—we make it easy for skilled digital workers to help refugees prosper. As a volunteer you can:

  • Contribute to one of our projects and/or
  • Mentor an apprentice

We have immediate need in the following disciplines:

  • Ethnography & User Research
  • Agile Project Management
  • Human-Centered Design
  • Web Development & Architecture
  • Social Media & Digital Marketing
  • Others*

*If your skillset isn't listed above and you'd like to get involved, please contact us. The need for passionate professionals with relevant skills is immense. We enjoy matching skilled workers with meaningful projects that make a significant impact on helping the refugee crisis.
