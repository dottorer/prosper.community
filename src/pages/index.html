Join The Team

Prosper is a diverse community of digital creatives collaborating to help refugees prosper in Europe.

The Situation

  • 59.5 million displaced people worldwide
  • 19.5 million refugees
  • $27 million spent per day on relief and aid for displaced people
  • Over 50,000 organizations are working to solve this problem in different ways.

The Problem

Organizations working on the crisis don't have the luxury to step back and create proactive solutions, they only have time to react.

This is where we as digital workers (who are not on the front-line) have an opportunity to help create positive change in a broken world...

The Solution

build local and global communities

We have a team of passionate and skilled professionals collaborating to solve the problems of the refugee crisis from across the globe. We're using Slack to communicate in real-time and we are growing fast.

If you're interested in starting an Prosper meetup in your city, send us a message.

discover problems

We are currently researching the problems that refugees, refugee representatives and organizations like Refugees Welcome are facing amidst the crisis. We are interviewing founders and workers in organizations all over the world and discussing the problems we discover with our team on the Slack channel.

pick a first problem

We have chosen our first problem to solve:

Projects doing meaningful work within the refugee crisis are scattered all across the web. This causes a major issue:

  • This makes it difficult for refugees, volunteers, and funders to find exactly what they are looking for.

solve the problem

We are currently working on a solution to this problem. We have a prototype up and running: RefugeeProjects.com to seed an initial database of meaningful projects across the refugee crisis.

If you're interested to see how the project is progressing, you can take a look at our Trello board: Prosper RefugeeProjects

document process

As we solve the problem, we are documenting our process so that we can empower other teams who want to solve a specific problem of the refugee crisis.

launch apprenticeship

In February we plan to launch the first digital apprenticeship for refugees. We're teaming up with Refugees On Rails to help graduates from their first cohort get real-world collaborative coding experience working on Prosper projects. We're currently raising funds to build a scholarship bursary for the apprenticeship and would love to speak with anyone interested in this program.

Potential apprentices must have the ability to:

  • Read and write HTML, CSS, Javascript and Ruby
  • Build CRUD applications in Rails provided project specifications
  • Collaborate using Git and Github

We're going to pair our apprentices with senior Ruby developers who will mentor apprentices through working on project tasks and collaborating with an interdisciplinary team.
