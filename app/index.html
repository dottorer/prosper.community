About

Refugee Community is a free online platform for people who want to take action and help refugees. Whether you are looking to create a startup, setup a charity, host a refugee in your home, or simply want to learn how to get involved—this is a community for people who have heard the cry of our fellow humans and have determined to come together and do whatever we can to help them.

The Refugee Situation

  • By the end of 2014, there were 19.5 million refugees worldwide. This number is growing.[1]
  • There are 4.1 million Syrians who have sought refuge in other countries since 2011. [2]
  • As of today, 499,826 Syrian refugees have reached Europe this year.[3]
  • Only 2.2% of Syrian refugees have been offered places to resettle in 2015. [4]
